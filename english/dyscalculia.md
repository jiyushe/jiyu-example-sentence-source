# dyscalculia

<p><span class="chinese">另一些人，他们在处理数学问题上存在障碍，则可能患有计算困难。</span><span class="english">Someone who has trouble with mathematics may have  dyscalculia.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">患有计算障碍症的孩子在读数和记住数字方面有困难。</span><span class="english">Children with dyscalculia have trouble reading numbers and picturing them in their mind.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">大部分正常人都可以轻松自如地处理数字。但有计算障碍的人就不行了。</span><span class="english">Most people process numbers very easily ?almost automatically ?but people with dyscalculia do not.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">计算障碍症意思就是“数数很差”，这个意思有点讽刺，因为每个得了这个病的人需要经常数数。</span><span class="english">Dyscalculia means “counting badly” which is ironic since those who suffer from it need to count often.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">根据研究结果显示，计算障碍是遗传而来，特殊情况也可能是大脑异常导致的。</span><span class="english">According to researchers dyscalculia is inherited, and specific abnormalities in the brain might be a cause.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">这可能有助于解释为什么儿童在数字文化中有发展性计算障碍觉得如此艰难来学习算术。</span><span class="english">This may help explain why children in numerate cultures with developmental dyscalculia find it so difficult to learn arithmetic.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">专家指出，有计算障碍的学生完成作业需要更多时间。S同时建议，应该允许他们使用计算器学习。</span><span class="english">Experts say students with dyscalculia need extra time to complete their work. S also advises let ting them work with a calculator in school.</span></p>
