# brahmin

<p><span class="chinese">三时，优陀耶婆罗门，以白饭盛满世尊之钵。</span><span class="english">The Brahmin Udaya filled the Blessed One's bowl with rice.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">他住在巴拉纳戈尔，是一位婆罗门学者的儿子。</span><span class="english">He lived at Baranagore and was the son of a brahmin pundit.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">最高瓦尔纳是婆罗门，牧师和学者。</span><span class="english">The highest varna is the Brahmin , the priests and scholars.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">一位婆罗门习惯每天用食物贡品来敬拜他的家庭神。</span><span class="english">A brahmin used to worship his Family Deity daily with food offerings.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">婆罗门又回家换掉了这身华丽的衣服。</span><span class="english">Once again, the Brahmin returned to his home and changed his clothes.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">萨西和萨拉特是一对表兄弟，来自加尔各答一个虔诚的婆罗门家庭。</span><span class="english">Sashi and Sarat were two cousins who came from a pious brahmin family of Calcutta.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">我不能因为你是婆罗门或属于任何其他的阶层就谴责你。</span><span class="english">I cannot condemn you because you are a Brahmin or belong to some other blinking caste.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">如果你想去卡大哈的婆罗门社区，首先第一步必须要去卡大哈。</span><span class="english">If you want to go to the brahmin quarter of Khardaha, you must first of all go to Khardaha.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">五时，世尊闻孙陀利迦婆罗堕婆阇婆罗门之足音，摘去头包。</span><span class="english">The Blessed One hearing the foot steps of the Brahmin Sundarika Bharadvaja disclosed his head.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">最初被阿难拦截，佛陀无意中打听到，叫那位婆罗门到他这一边。</span><span class="english">Being earlier turned away by Ananda , Buddha overheard this and called the Brahmin to his side.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">二时，萦髻婆罗堕婆阇婆罗门，来诣世尊处。诣而与世尊互相致问，坐于一面。</span><span class="english">The Brahmin Jañà Bharadvaja approached the Blessed One, exchanged friendly greetings and sat on a side.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">人们好奇地聚拢了过来，最后也都开始朝衣服膜拜，因为婆罗门在这样做。</span><span class="english">The people gathered round in wonder. And in time came to worship the clothes as this Brahmin was doing.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">二时，不害婆罗堕婆阇婆罗门，来诣世尊处。诣而与世尊互相致问，坐于一面。</span><span class="english">The Brahmin Ahimsaka Bharadvaja approached the Blessed One, exchanged friendly greetings and sat on a side.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">他试图结合起来，统一基督教形式与婆罗门概念的最高个人的上帝。</span><span class="english">He tried to combine a Unitarian form of Christianity with the Brahmin conception of the supreme personal God.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">二时，孝养婆罗门来诣世尊处。诣已，与世尊互相致问，坐于一面。</span><span class="english">Then the brahmin who nourished his mother approached the Blessed One, exchanged friendly greetings and sat on a side.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">一如是我闻。尔时，世尊住摩揭陀国南山一苇婆罗门村。</span><span class="english">I heard thus. At one time the Blessed One was living in the Brahmin village Ekanaliya in the southern hills of Magadha.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">七时，提婆比多婆罗门，施一担温水、一瓶糖蜜予尊者优波婆那。</span><span class="english">The Brahmin Devahita gave venerable Upavana some warm water with a pingo and a man to carry it and some molasses in a bag.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">三其时，库摩都萨邑之婆罗门与家主等，因事而集聚于集会堂。雨绵绵下降。</span><span class="english">At that time the Brahmin householders of the hamlet Khomadussa had gathered for some purpose and drops rain were also falling.</span></p>

<p><span class="chinese">五其时，尊者梵天之母婆罗门女，常以供牲供梵天。</span><span class="english">At that time venerable Brahmadeva's mother, a Brahmin woman was steadfastly making adoration to Brahma with out stretched arms.</span></p>
